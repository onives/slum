About SLUM

Serving Lives Under Marginalization (SLUM) is a grass-root organization established in 2016 by and for female sex workers envisioning communities where the marginalized populations access and utilize sexual and reproductive health rights and services in a friendly manner through strengthening HIV prevention, access to non-discriminatory sexual and reproductive health rights and services, strategic partnerships and advocacy for the marginalized populations.
